About

Nesto is a platform exploring aesthetics, functionality, and craftsmanship design for your space.

Nesto was established in 2022. We are headquartered in Hanoi, and have offices and showroom across Vietnam.

Founded in 2010, Lambert & Fils is a contemporary lighting studio that creates original collections and collaborates with emerging designers around the world. Preserving the bond between design and making, all lighting is handmade in the Lambert & Fils atelier in the heart of Montreal.

We handpick leading contemporary designers who are strong interpreters of our philosophy and combine their talents with the passionate Nesto team.
